1 1. Quanteda tutorial

I reviewed the quanteda tutorial and used the main workflow from it in this assignment: building a corpus, converting text into tokens, and then creating a document-feature matrix (dfm) for analysis. This workflow is useful because it turns raw text into a structure that can be compared across documents.

Answer for 2a(i):
There are both similarities and differences among presidents and across time. The main similarity is that inaugural speeches consistently focus on public leadership, government, and the nation. The main difference is historical emphasis. Earlier presidents use more formal constitutional and institutional language, while twentieth- and twenty-first-century presidents use more modern language about America, democracy, the economy, and the world.

4 3. What is Wordfish?

Wordfish is an unsupervised one-dimensional text scaling method. It uses word frequencies to estimate the position of each document on a latent dimension. In simple terms, it places documents on a line based on how their word choices differ. Documents with similar language are placed close together, and documents with different language are placed farther apart. It is commonly used to scale political texts such as legislative speeches or party manifestos along an ideological or thematic dimension without requiring any prior labeling of documents.
